About me

This is me

Hello, I'm Michael Jeffrey. I originally got into web development shortly after I started school at the Art Institute of Phoenix for Web Design and Interactive Media.

I was thrilled with the ability to not only design websites, but manipulate data into something that people can actually use. Which led me to want to start making application that people don't just use to consume data, but input data.

My dream is to work for a company making functional applications that people can make a part of their everyday lives. And privacy policy willing, collect data that can really speak fluently about the users.
